Privacy & Cookies

Voiceover AI Hub (the “Site”) publishes independent guides about AI voice tools. We use Google Analytics 4 (“GA4”) to understand how visitors discover and read this content. This page explains what data is processed, why we use it, and how you can exercise GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and LGPD rights.

Summary of data we collect

  • Page views, session duration, referrers, and device/browser data captured by GA4 when you allow analytics cookies.
  • Approximate IP address used by Google to derive high-level location (country/region). IPs are discarded before we can access them.
  • No ads, remarketing pixels, account logins, or contact forms are hosted on the Site.

How we use analytics data

We rely on aggregate GA4 reports to:

  • Measure which tutorials are most useful and plan future content.
  • Troubleshoot traffic drops or broken links.
  • Understand the mix of devices and languages our audience uses.

Analytics cookies only load after you opt in through the consent banner. Essential cookies are not set by the Site today.

Legal bases and regional notices

European Economic Area & UK (GDPR)

Our legal basis for analytics is consent (GDPR art. 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw consent at any time by selecting “Cookie settings” and disabling analytics.

Your GDPR rights include: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Brazil (LGPD)

We follow the same consent-based approach required by LGPD art. 7(I). Data subject rights (access, correction, anonymization, portability, deletion, and revocation of consent) are available via the contact email below.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

We treat GA4 data as “sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising” only if analytics cookies are enabled, and we currently disable advertising features.

We do not “sell” personal information. If you wish to exercise California privacy rights or opt out of any future selling or sharing, email us or disable analytics in the cookie settings.

Retention, storage, and transfers

  • GA4 retains event data for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Data is stored by Google on servers located in the United States or other regions listed in Google’s documentation.
  • We do not combine analytics data with other datasets or export raw logs.

Managing cookies & consent

You can make or change your choice at any time:

  • Use the button to reopen the banner.
  • Adjust your browser settings to block analytics cookies altogether.
  • Email us if you need help revoking consent or want a record of prior decisions.

Contact

Email privacy@voiceoveraihub.com for any privacy or data-subject requests. Please describe the right you wish to exercise and provide enough detail so we can locate your consent record.
